Introduction

ecoTrak is a smart emissions monitoring system that empowers users to track carbon monoxide (CO) and hydrocarbon (HC) levels emitted from vehicles. It helps raise environmental awareness and supports decision-making through real-time data analytics and historical trends.

Objectives

  • Tracking vehicle emissions in real time
  • Storing emission data in the cloud for future analysis
  • Alerting when levels exceed safety limits
  • Promoting eco-friendly transportation practices

Mission

To provide a reliable, accessible, and intelligent solution that enables individuals and authorities to monitor, analyze, and reduce vehicular emissions effectively.

Vision

To drive the transition toward a sustainable future where technology actively supports environmental protection and public health through smart monitoring and data-driven insights.
